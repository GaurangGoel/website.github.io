Hello,

My name is Gaurang Goel, and I am a sophomore studying Computer Science at University of Texas at Dallas, as well as an upcoming Esports Professional in the fields of coaching, analysis, and design; specifically in the game Clash of Clans.

My passion is to find creative solutions for unique problems, whether it is finding the shortest way to write a line of code, solving programming errors, or creating new pathway for my team in Esports.
